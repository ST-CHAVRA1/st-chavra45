Welcome to ST. CHAVARA SCHOOL

"Empowering Minds. Enriching Lives"

Nestled in the lush, picturesque terrains of Saradwadi, Shirur, Maharashtra, St. Chavara School was inaugurated on 13th June 2019 by the Catholic Diocese of Kalyan. The School is managed by the Santhome Charitable Trust of Kalyan.
